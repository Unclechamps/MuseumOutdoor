A Splash Of Color

Welcome to Museum Outdoor, where our mission is to help enthusiasts, lovers, and even casual admirers find local street art and engage with the artists responsible for it. We created MO as a platform of expression driven by a communal ideal built to challenge modern art and society. We firmly believe in the extraordinary people behind the murals that decorate our cities and think that they deserve to be recognized for their inspiring contributions.

Graffiti No More

Street art has evolved from its early days as a counter-cultural form of expression to cement its relevance in the global art scene. Despite becoming “mainstream,” the soul behind street art has remained raw and true beckoning the attention and appreciation of all, no matter the individual's background.

100% Free

The best thing about street art is that it’s free and open to all, like all great art should be. There’s no right or wrong way to create or enjoy it, but when done well, street art invokes deep emotions in its observers, creating a truly visceral and immersive experience.

Artists Behind The Artworks

World-renown street artists like Banksy, C215, and Above have amassed a great deal of recognition and legitimized the artform. However, for every Banksy there are hundreds, maybe thousands, of artists worldwide that go largely unrecognized and MO is working hard to change that, one city at a time

Commissions

Whether you are simply a lover of street art like us, looking to commission a mural of your own, a photographer wanting to add to your portfolio, or a starving artist in need of this months rent, MO can connect you with the right people to make sure your work finds the right eyes.
